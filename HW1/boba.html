Bubble Tea

One may say that you are missing out if you never tried bubble tea. The traditional bubble tea started as milk tea with tapioca, which has it's sweet and chewy all in one. Upon walking into a bubble tea shop today, you may see catergories such as milk tea, slush, fruit tea, coffee, milk shake, etc. With so many varieties, you will never go back to the plain old boring tea ever again. Among these drinks comes with topping which are put into your drinks. Toppings vary from chewy tapioca to bursting sweet boba. If you have yet to try, below are the best bubble tea shops ranked with their best tasting drinks!
