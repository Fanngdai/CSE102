Taiwan is a very fun place to go to for vacation. There are many night markets with very good food. The MRT (subway) is very convenient and cheap. It gets majority northern part of Taiwan (Taipei). To ride the MRT, you will neeed to buy a subway card from a convenient store (seven eleven) and fill it up with some change. When you take the MRT, you just have to put the metro card next to the entrance, the door will open to let you in. There are about a night market every few stations away. The food usually cost under three dollars per item. The portions can usually fill you up quickly so remember to bring a friend with you so you can eat more options!
